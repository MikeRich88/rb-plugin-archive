RbImageAxisTransについて

画像の水平、垂直の反転、時計回り、反時計回り、角度を指定して回転などができます。REALbasic 2.1用のpluginです。

(1.1.0->1.1.1)

  • mic_Rotateの画像が微妙にずれるバグを修正

(1.0.0->1.1.0)

  • Windowsに対応
  • mic_Rotateの引数変更
  • mic_Rotateの速度アップ
  • 2.1用
  • 名前変更

使い方

水平方向に反転

p.mic_FlipH as Picture

p: Picture

垂直方向に反転

p.mic_FlipV as Picture

p: Picture

時計方向に90度回転

p.mic_Rotate90 as Picture

p.mic_RotateRight as Picture

p: Picture

反時計方向に90度回転

p.mic_Rotate270 as Picture

p.mic_RotateLeft as Picture

p: Picture

180度回転

p.mic_Rotate180 as Picture

p: Picture

回転

p.mic_Rotate( Angle as Double, RotateCenterX as Double, RotateCenterY as Double, SrcPicLeft as Integer, SrcPicTop as Integer, DstPicWidth as Integer, DstPicHeight as Integer, BackColor as Color ) as Picture

p: Picture

エラー

mic_GetImageTransError as Integer
